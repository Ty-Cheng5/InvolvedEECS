Are you a new student at Michigan majoring in the EECS department?

Do you not know where to look for opportunities outside your classes?

Look no further! InvolvedEECS has your back.

InvolvedEECS is a site where users can upload, teach, learn, inform, and provide different kinds of EECS related service at the University of Michigan's campus. Whether you want research or help on what to do for your next exam? InvolvedEECS acts as all-in-one hub to share different kinds of resources.
